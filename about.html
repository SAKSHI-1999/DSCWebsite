About Us

Developer Student Clubs (DSCs) is a Google Developers program for university students.

DSC VIT Vellore is a non-profit student developer group aimed to develop, learn and share. Open to any student, ranging from novice developers who are just starting, to advanced developers who want to further their skills, it is intended to be a space for students to learn and collaborate as they solve problems concentrated on mobile and web development.
