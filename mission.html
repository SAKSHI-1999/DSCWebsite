Our Mission

Colleges are not known to provide platforms to learn and develop projects. Many students have ideas and talent, but have not acquired enough skills to implement these ideas. We, at DSC VIT Vellore aim to provide students with a chance to develop skills and learn by interaction. We hope to provide budding developers with a platform where they can learn and explore and more so, show off their talent.
